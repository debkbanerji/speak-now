Speak Now is a social network which is centered around sound rather than text

You can record and share short snippets of yourself speaking, or singing, if that's more your style.
If you're less musically inclined or not a fan of talking you can also just share Spotify links

Idea by Griffin Dufek

Speak Now is Open Source
